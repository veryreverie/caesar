'structure.dat' file format:

* Line 1.         The word 'Lattice'.
* Lines 2 to 4.   Cartesian lattice vectors in bohr.
* Line 5.         The word 'Atoms'.
* Lines 6 to N.   Atomic symbol, atomic mass, and Cartesian atomic positions in bohr.
* Line N+1.       The word 'Symmetry'.
* Line N+2 to M.  Symmetry operations.
* Line M+1.       The word 'End'.
